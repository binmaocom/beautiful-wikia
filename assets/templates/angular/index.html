Welcome to our 2013 Winter Hackathon project

For our hack, we decided to build a version of the Wikia article experience that explores new ideas for improving readability, content suggestion, sense of community while also offering more opportunities for immersive and higher value ad experiences.

Check out our hack on live production data by visiting the following articles:

  • Batman Wiki - Batman
  • Walking Dead Wikia - Dead Weight
  • Shadow of Mordor Wikia - Mordor
  • GTA Wikia - Wei Cheng
  • Call of Duty Wikia - World at War

To begin the experience on your own, pick an article from Wikia and copy the URL from your browser's address bar. The URL should look something like this:

http://batman.wikia.com/wiki/Batman

Then, hit the ? key on your keyboard. Paste your URL and hit Return and voila! From there, feel free the navigate the article links just like you normally would. Enjoy!
